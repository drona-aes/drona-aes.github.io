About Us

Drona Academy of Engineering Studies, located in Chengannur, exclusively conducts B'Tech tuition for all universities and branches enabling students to excel in their respective university exams. Our methodology includes question paper discussion, problem solving and assessment of syllabus. One module for a day is what we aim without compromising the quality of education. Assessment of syllabus and classes based on that is our specialization.

All Universities & Branches

At Drona we conduct classes for all universities and branches. Students are given individual attention and complete understanding of the topics is guaranteed. Classes are designed in such a way that each module is completed in a day. This enables students to take the full benefit of the class with least time. Extra care is taken in conducting classes irrespective of the background of students.

Experienced Faculty

Our highly experienced faulty is having in-depth knowledge in engineering subjects. They are capable of delivering quality classes while handling multiple entities. Faculties at Drona is keen in updating their knowledge with the changing syllabus as well as the changing trends in engineering technologies. Exam oriented sessions are taken along with regular class.

Assessment of Syllabus

Apart from taking classes based on the assessment of syllabus, discussion of previous question papers is an integral part of success in university exams. At Drona we don't let any stones unturned. Sessions solely based on solving and discussing question papers are held. This ensures a greater success for the students of Drona.
